how does it work?

year in film colors transforms your film preferences throughout the year into a dynamic visual representation tailored just for you. but how does it work exactly?
the process begins with a dive into your letterboxd diary based on the username and year you initially provide. each page of your diary is meticulously examined to gather the details of the films you've watched over the year. a key part of this involves identifying the dominant color of each film's poster. these dominant colors are crucial for coloring the corresponding day on your personalized calendar. each calendar cell can be hovered over to reveal the related film names and viewing dates. if you've watched more than one film in a day, the dominant colors from each poster are blended to create a unique color for that day. the end result? a one-of-a-kind colorful calendar that represents your annual film journey.
